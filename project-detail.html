Project 01 / CREA

CREA Unified Digital Platform

Central Railway Engineers Association needed a secure and unified digital system to replace fragmented membership, communication, and document workflows. We delivered a production platform with structured modules for events, forum, circulars, manuals, court cases, suggestions, and role-based administration.

Problem Context

CREA operations were distributed across disconnected channels, creating delays in communication, reduced transparency, and high manual coordination overhead.

Delivery Scope

Membership lifecycle management, payment integration, event/news publishing, forum moderation, digital documents, and administrative analytics dashboard.

Detailed Outcomes

The platform created a single operational source of truth for members and administrators. Project 03 / LOCO-INFO

Loco-Info Production System

We developed Loco-Info, a production system digitizing locomotive maintenance for Electric Loco Shed, Bhusawal. The solution serves 400+ railway staff across 50+ units with a Flutter front-end, role-based access, and real-time database architecture. A dedicated streaming pipeline reduced retrieval windows from 30 minutes to under 5 seconds with 99%+ uptime.

Operational Challenge

Maintenance teams needed faster operational visibility across shifts, but legacy workflows relied on delayed records and manual retrieval paths.

System Capability

Real-time database updates, role-aware workflows, and recent-activity logs created a reliable operational timeline for supervisors and field engineers.

Detailed Outcomes

Loco-Info moved maintenance intelligence from delayed record-checking to live, action-ready status monitoring. Teams now use faster data retrieval, clearer accountability through the latest 100 activity events, and high-uptime operations to execute maintenance decisions with lower latency and higher confidence.

Insights

  • Developed Loco-Info for locomotive maintenance serving 400+ railway staff across 50+ units.
  • Implemented Flutter front-end with role-based access and a real-time database.
  • Included a rolling log of the 100 most recent operational activities.
  • Reduced retrieval time from 30 minutes to under 5 seconds, with 99%+ uptime.

Built to improve real-time visibility, shift handovers, and decision speed during active locomotive maintenance cycles.
